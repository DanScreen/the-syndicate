## Description (4,000 chars — draft, ~1,400)

> **Your Mates. One Acca. Every Leg Counts.**
>
> Tiki Acca turns the group-chat accumulator into a proper competition.
> Everyone in your group picks one leg. When the last pick lands, the acca
> locks with the best combined odds across UK bookmakers — and the scoreboard
> takes it from there.
>
> **HOW IT WORKS**
> • Start a group and share one invite link
> • Each member picks a leg — live odds from real bookmakers
> • The acca locks at first kickoff with the best combined odds
> • Results roll in, points are scored, and the leaderboard never lies
>
> **BUILT FOR GROUPS**
> • Group chat with reactions for the big moments (and the bad picks)
> • Leaderboards and stats that show who actually knows their football
> • Performance tracking across every group you're in
> • Push alerts when your acca locks, wins, or goes down swinging
>
> **THE IMPORTANT BIT**
> We're not a bookmaker. Tiki Acca never takes bets or payments — if your
> group wants to back the acca for real, one tap opens the betslip at a
> licensed UK bookmaker, and the bet is between you and them. We keep the
> score.
>
> 18+ only. UK only. Please gamble responsibly — visit BeGambleAware.org for
> free, confidential support.
>
> Questions? tikiacca@outlook.com
