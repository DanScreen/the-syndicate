WHAT TIKI ACCA IS

Tiki Acca is a social score-keeping app for private groups of friends who
follow football. Each member of a group picks one leg of a shared accumulator
("acca"); the app tracks the picks, shows live bookmaker odds, and keeps
points and leaderboards for bragging rights. Points have no monetary value.

NOT A GAMBLING OPERATOR

- No bets, wagers, or payments are accepted or processed anywhere in the app.
- There are no in-app purchases and no virtual currency. The app is free.
- Buttons such as "Open betslip" open the website of an independent,
  UK-licensed bookmaker externally (in the browser). Any bet a user places
  there is made directly with that licensed operator, outside this app.
- Distribution is restricted to the United Kingdom, where the linked
  bookmakers are licensed by the Gambling Commission.

AGE AND RESPONSIBLE GAMBLING

- Sign-up requires date of birth; 18+ is enforced server-side.
- Age rating is set to 18+.
- Responsible gambling guidance (BeGambleAware) is shown in the app, with a
  link to begambleaware.org.

USER-GENERATED CONTENT (GROUP CHAT)

Chat exists only inside private, invite-only friend groups. Safeguards:
- Profanity filter on posting.
- Long-press any message to REPORT it (reports alert the moderation team).
- Long-press to BLOCK a member — their messages are hidden immediately;
  unblock from Account > Blocked members.
- Message deletion by the author and by the group owner.
- Contact: tikiacca@outlook.com (also linked in Account > Legal & support).

ACCOUNT DELETION

Account > Delete account permanently removes the user's personal data and
revokes all sessions (Guideline 5.1.1(v)).
