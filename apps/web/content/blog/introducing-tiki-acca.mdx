Every group acca we've ever been part of lived in the same place: a WhatsApp chat. A betslip screenshot, a "who's still not picked?", a spreadsheet someone made in August and abandoned by November, and an argument — always an argument — about whose leg let everyone down.

We built **Tiki Acca** to give that ritual a proper home.

## How it works

1. **Start a group** and share the invite link in your chat.
2. **Everyone picks one leg** — competition, fixture, market, selection — from live bookmaker odds.
3. **The acca locks** when everyone's in, at the best combined odds we can find across UK bookmakers.
4. **Results track live**, leg by leg, as the matches finish.
5. **The leaderboard keeps score** — who consistently delivers, and who lets the acca down.

Picks stay editable until the first kickoff, results settle automatically from official match data, and nobody — not even the group owner — can mark their own bet a winner. The scoreboard can't be gamed.

## What we are (and aren't)

We're the coordination layer: picks, locked odds, live results, points. We are **not** a bookmaker — we never hold money or place bets, and we will never sell you tips. We keep score; we don't tell you what to pick.

Betting is optional, too. Points and pride work just as well — the leaderboard doesn't care whether money moved.

## The name

Tiki-taka is football's great passing game: everyone touches the ball. In a group acca, everyone touches the bet — one leg each, no hiding. **Tiki Acca.**
